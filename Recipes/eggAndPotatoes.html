Eggs and Potatoes

Description

This recipe is dear and true to my heart. Its been made since I was a little boy by my grandma and I continue to make it today. It only consists of eggs and potatoes but is grilled to perfection and is very hearty. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs
  2. Fry the potatoes and mix in with eggs
  3. Add oil to pan and cook the omelette
  4. Serve and enjoy
